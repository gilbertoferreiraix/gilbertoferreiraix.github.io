Gilberto Ferreira (he/they) is a multidisciplinary curator and project manager with a background in multiple arts and cultural management. Based between Berlin and Hamburg, he has extensive experience leading artistic planning and producing award-winning cultural projects. His work focuses on fostering international partnerships and promoting cultural exchange, with an emphasis on the intersection of music, contemporary performance and digital media. Currently, he develops transnational artistic projects and collaborations.
